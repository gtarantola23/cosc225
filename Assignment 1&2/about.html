About Me

Overview

Hi! My name is Grace Tarantola, and I am from Mountain Lakes, NJ. I am a senior computer science and chemistry double major here at Amherst College

Background

I lived in Verona, NJ until I was about 3 years old, and then my family moved to Mountain Lakes, NJ. Here is some more about me:

  • I have a twin sister, and two step siblings
    • My twin is a senior chemistry and computer science major at Middlebury College
    • My step sister is a freshman pre-med student at University of New Hampshire
    • My step brother is a junior archeology major at Colgate College
  • I have four big dogs named: Ollie, Wyatt, Fiona and Phoebe, and they are all 100 lbs!
  • I graduated Mountain Lakes High school in 2019, and immediately attended Amherst College.
  • I am currently writing a chemistry thesis in the Durr Lab. It aims to create novel group V metal catalysts to aid in the ring opening polymerization of cyclic esters! I get to run my own experiments, read relevant research papers, and analyze all my data using various computer programs.
